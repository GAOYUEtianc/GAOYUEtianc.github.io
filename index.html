Toronto·Canada gao12@ualberta.ca

I am a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Keebo.AI, specializing in reinforcement learning for cloud data warehouse optimization. My expertise includes Reinforcement Learning, Machine Learning Theory, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and Large Language Models (LLMs). I’m passionate about applying advanced AI to real-world challenges in healthcare, education, and legal sectors, with a current focus on AI-driven litigation applications. If you’re interested in AI-driven ventures, I’d be excited to connect.

I earned my Thesis-Based Master's degree in the Computing Science Department at the University of Alberta, where I was supervised by Csaba Szepesvari in Reinforcement Learning and Bandits, and co-supervised by Or Sheffet in Differential Privacy. I have a strong passion for theoretical topics, and I find it inspiring to be part of the RLAI/AMII, where brilliant ideas from exceptional scholars and students abound.

I earned my B.Sc. in Mathematics from The Chinese University of Hong Kong. My time at CUHK was immensely meaningful, as I not only gained a wealth of knowledge but also became more self-motivated, rigorous, and efficient. Most importantly, I formed some of my most cherished friendships during those four years.
